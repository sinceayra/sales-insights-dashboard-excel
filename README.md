# Vrinda Store Sales Dashboard | Excel Project

## Project Overview

This project presents an interactive sales dashboard built in Microsoft Excel using Vrinda Store's annual sales data. The dashboard transforms raw transactional data into meaningful business insights through Pivot Tables, Pivot Charts, KPIs, and interactive slicers.

The goal of this project is to analyze sales performance, customer demographics, product categories, and sales channels to support data-driven business decisions.

---

## Business Objective

Vrinda Store wanted to understand its sales performance and customer purchasing behavior to identify growth opportunities and improve decision-making.

This dashboard helps answer key business questions related to:

- Sales performance throughout the year
- Customer demographics
- Product category performance
- Sales channel effectiveness
- Order status trends

---

## Key Insights

### Customer Insights
- Women contribute the majority of sales.
- Adult customers are the largest customer segment.

### Sales Channel Insights
- Amazon generates the highest number of orders.
- Myntra and Flipkart are among the top-performing channels.

### Product Insights
- Sets and Kurtas are the best-selling product categories.
- Ethnic wear contributes significantly to total sales.

### Order Performance
- Most orders are successfully delivered.
- Returns, cancellations, and refunds account for a small percentage of total orders.

---

## Business Recommendations

- Focus marketing campaigns on female customers, as they represent the largest customer segment.
- Maintain adequate inventory levels for high-performing categories such as Sets and Kurtas.
- Strengthen partnerships with top-performing sales channels like Amazon and Myntra.
- Investigate reasons for returned and cancelled orders to improve customer satisfaction.
- Develop targeted promotions for underperforming product categories.
